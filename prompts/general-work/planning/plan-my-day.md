# Plan My Day

Use this to build a realistic daily schedule.

## Prompt

Create a realistic plan for my day.

Available time: `[start time to end time]`

Fixed commitments: `[meetings, appointments, or deadlines]`

Tasks: `[task list with estimates if known]`

Energy or focus constraints: `[details]`

Prioritize the most valuable outcome, include breaks and transition time, and
leave a small buffer for surprises. If everything cannot fit, explicitly defer
the least important items rather than overloading the schedule.
