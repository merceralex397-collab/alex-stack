# Evaluate an AI Response

Use this before relying on an important generated answer.

## Prompt

Evaluate the AI response below against the original request.

Original request:

`[paste request]`

Response:

`[paste response]`

Relevant evidence or criteria:

`[paste sources, rubric, or known facts]`

Check instruction-following, factual support, completeness, internal
consistency, safety, and usefulness. Identify unsupported claims and important
omissions. Distinguish confirmed errors from concerns that require verification.
Finish with a verdict of **usable**, **usable with changes**, or **not reliable**,
followed by the smallest set of corrections needed.
