# Teach Me a Topic

Use this to learn something through explanation and practice.

## Prompt

Teach me `[topic]`.

What I already know: `[background]`

Why I want to learn it: `[purpose]`

Preferred depth: `[quick overview, practical competence, or deep understanding]`

Start with a plain-language mental model, then build the idea in small steps
using concrete examples. Point out common misconceptions. Finish with a short
practice exercise and questions that check understanding rather than recall.
