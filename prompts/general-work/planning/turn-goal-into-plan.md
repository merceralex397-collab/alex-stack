# Turn a Goal into a Plan

Use this to convert a broad outcome into executable work.

## Prompt

Turn this goal into a practical plan:

`[goal]`

Current situation: `[starting point]`

Deadline or time horizon: `[date or duration]`

Available resources: `[time, people, tools, or budget]`

Constraints: `[limits and non-negotiables]`

Break the work into milestones and concrete next actions. Show dependencies,
risks, and a simple definition of done. Put the smallest useful first action at
the top and avoid unnecessary process.
