# Respond to an Incident

Use this to structure investigation and communication during an operational
incident.

## Prompt

Help respond to this incident in `[system or service]`:

`[symptoms and alerts]`

Start time and timeline: `[known events]`

User or business impact: `[impact]`

Recent changes: `[deployments, configuration, traffic, or dependencies]`

Available telemetry: `[logs, metrics, traces, dashboards]`

Operational constraints: `[access, safety, compliance, or coordination]`

Prioritize user safety and service stabilization. Separate confirmed facts from
hypotheses, build a concise timeline, rank likely causes, and recommend
reversible diagnostic or mitigation actions with success and rollback criteria.
Track decisions and evidence. Do not make destructive changes or claim
resolution without observing recovery. After stabilization, outline follow-up
for root cause, prevention, and documentation.
