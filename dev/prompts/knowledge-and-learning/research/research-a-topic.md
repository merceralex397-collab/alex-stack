# Research a Topic

Use this for a structured, evidence-aware overview.

## Prompt

Research `[topic or question]` for `[purpose]`.

Scope: `[what to include and exclude]`

Audience level: `[beginner, informed, or expert]`

Important constraints: `[date range, region, source types, or other limits]`

Explain the current consensus, important disagreements, strongest supporting
evidence, uncertainties, and practical implications. Prefer primary and
authoritative sources, cite every time-sensitive or consequential claim, and
separate sourced facts from your own inference.
