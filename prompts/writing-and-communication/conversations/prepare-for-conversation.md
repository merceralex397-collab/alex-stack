# Prepare for a Conversation

Use this before a difficult, important, or sensitive conversation.

## Prompt

Help me prepare for a conversation with `[person or group]` about `[topic]`.

Context: `[background]`

My desired outcome: `[outcome]`

Their likely concerns: `[concerns, if known]`

Boundaries or sensitivities: `[details]`

Suggest a respectful opening, the essential points to communicate, useful
questions to ask, and ways to respond to likely reactions. Include an outcome I
can accept if my preferred result is not available. Avoid manipulative tactics.
