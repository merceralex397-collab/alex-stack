# Develop an Idea

Use this to turn an interesting seed into a clearer concept.

## Prompt

Develop this idea: `[idea]`.

Intended audience: `[audience]`

Desired effect: `[what it should achieve or evoke]`

Constraints: `[limits]`

Explore the idea's central promise, distinctive features, tensions, and likely
weaknesses. Propose three different directions it could take, then develop the
strongest direction into a concise concept with a practical next experiment.
