# Generate Ideas

Use this to explore a wide range of possibilities before narrowing.

## Prompt

Generate ideas for `[challenge or opportunity]`.

Goal: `[desired outcome]`

Audience or users: `[who it is for]`

Constraints: `[time, budget, medium, rules, or things to avoid]`

Existing ideas or references: `[optional]`

First generate a varied set, including safe, unusual, and hybrid directions.
Then group them by theme, select the three most promising against the stated
goal, and suggest a cheap way to test each one.
