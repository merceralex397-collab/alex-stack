# Give Constructive Feedback

Use this to make feedback specific, fair, and actionable.

## Prompt

Help me give constructive feedback about `[work or behavior]`.

Recipient and relationship: `[context]`

What I observed: `[specific facts]`

Impact: `[why it matters]`

Desired change or outcome: `[request]`

Draft feedback that distinguishes observation from interpretation, recognizes
relevant strengths, explains the impact, and makes a clear, achievable request.
Keep the tone direct and respectful.
