# Make a Decision

Use this when the decision is unclear or feels stuck.

## Prompt

Help me make this decision: `[decision]`.

Context: `[background]`

Desired outcome: `[what success looks like]`

Constraints: `[time, budget, commitments, values, or other limits]`

Known options: `[options, if any]`

First identify the few factors that genuinely drive the decision. Then give me
a recommendation, the reasoning behind it, the main downside, and the next
reversible step I can take. Ask only questions whose answers could materially
change the recommendation.
