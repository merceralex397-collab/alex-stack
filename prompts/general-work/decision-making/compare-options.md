# Compare Options

Use this to compare several choices against the same criteria.

## Prompt

I need to choose between `[option A]`, `[option B]`, and `[other options]`.

Context: `[relevant situation]`

My goal: `[desired outcome]`

Important criteria: `[cost, time, risk, quality, flexibility, or other criteria]`

Constraints: `[limits or non-negotiables]`

Compare the options in a table. Explain the meaningful trade-offs, identify
missing information that could change the decision, and recommend an option.
State your assumptions and confidence. Do not invent facts.
