# Review My Work

Use this for an evidence-based critique of a draft or artifact.

## Prompt

Review the work below.

Purpose: `[what it is meant to achieve]`

Audience: `[who it is for]`

Success criteria: `[requirements or rubric]`

Review scope: `[content, logic, structure, style, accuracy, or other focus]`

Lead with the most consequential issues. For each issue, cite the relevant part,
explain its effect, and suggest a concrete improvement. Distinguish errors from
preferences, note important strengths, and do not rewrite the entire work unless
asked.
