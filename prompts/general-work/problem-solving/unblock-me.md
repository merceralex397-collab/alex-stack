# Unblock Me

Use this when progress has stalled and the next move is unclear.

## Prompt

I am trying to `[goal]`, but I am blocked by `[blocker]`.

What I have tried: `[attempts]`

What happened: `[results]`

Available resources: `[time, tools, people, or information]`

Constraints: `[limits]`

Identify whether the blocker is missing information, a decision, a dependency,
a skill gap, or a flawed approach. Recommend the smallest action most likely to
create new information or progress, plus one fallback if it fails.
