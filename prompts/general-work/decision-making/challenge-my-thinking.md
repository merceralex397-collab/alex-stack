# Challenge My Thinking

Use this to test an idea before committing to it.

## Prompt

Act as a constructive skeptic. Examine the following idea or belief:

`[idea or belief]`

Context: `[relevant background]`

Identify:

- assumptions I may be treating as facts;
- plausible counterarguments and failure modes;
- evidence that would support or weaken the idea;
- perspectives or stakeholders I may have missed;
- the strongest revised version of the idea.

Be candid but practical. Distinguish facts, inferences, and speculation.
