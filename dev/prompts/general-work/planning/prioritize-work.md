# Prioritize Work

Use this to decide what to do now, later, delegate, or drop.

## Prompt

Help me prioritize the following work:

`[task list]`

My main objective: `[objective]`

Deadlines and commitments: `[details]`

Available capacity: `[time or people]`

Assess each task by impact, urgency, effort, dependency, and cost of delay.
Return a ranked list grouped into **do now**, **schedule**, **delegate**, and
**drop or defer**. Flag tasks that look urgent but do not meaningfully support
the objective.
