# Brief an AI Assistant

Use this to give an AI assistant a clear, bounded assignment.

## Prompt

Complete this task: `[task]`.

Context: `[background and why the task matters]`

Desired outcome: `[what success looks like]`

In scope: `[included work]`

Out of scope: `[excluded work]`

Constraints: `[rules, tools, time, safety, or style requirements]`

Source material: `[files, links, data, or pasted content]`

Deliverable: `[format and location]`

Verification: `[checks that should pass]`

Inspect the available evidence before making assumptions. Preserve unrelated
work, label uncertainty, and report the result, verification performed, and
anything that remains unresolved.
